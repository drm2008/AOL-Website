Research Methods & Applied Statistics

AN OVERVIEW & LEARNING OUTCOMES

Research Foundations & Philosophy

Explores the philosophical bedrock of scholarly inquiry, distinguishing between deductive and inductive reasoning and the "Hourglass Notion."

Research Methods

Covers techniques for qualitative, quantitative, and mixed-methods designs, including ethical considerations and methodology selection.

Proposal and Thesis Writing

Focuses on articulating research problems, academic writing styles, citing sources, and defining study boundaries through assumptions and limitations.

Applied Statistics

Application of statistical techniques to real-world data, including descriptive and inferential statistics for informed decision-making.

SPSS Training

Comprehensive training on SPSS for data management, running procedures, and interpreting complex graphical data representations.
